About Our School

A legacy of learning and service since 1943

Founded on July 5, 1943, under the guidance of Ven.Balangoda Ananda Maithreeya Thero, our school began its journey as the “Miriswatte Sri Dharmananda English School.”

With the blessings of the local community and just twenty pioneering students, it stood as a humble yet powerful beginning—built with dedication, simplicity, and a vision for education that continues to inspire generations.

A Message from Our Revered Founder

Honoring the vision and guidance of our founder, whose wisdom continues to inspire every generation of learners.

In 1943, our beloved founder, Ven. Balangoda Ananda Maithreeya Thero, established this institution with a noble vision — to create a place of learning that unites knowledge, discipline, and compassion. What began as a humble classroom under the shade of coconut leaves has grown into a center of excellence and service for generations of students. Guided by his principles of mindfulness, moral strength, and respect for all beings, our school continues to shape young minds with the same spirit of wisdom and kindness that inspired its founding.

– In remembrance of Ven. Balangoda Ananda Maithreeya Thero

Founder, R/Balangoda Ananda Maithreeya Central College
